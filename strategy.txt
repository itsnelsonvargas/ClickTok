- Bundle deals and "Buy 1 Get 1" offers

Content Strategy:
- Always show price in BOTH PHP and USD if possible

---------------------------------------------------

- Weekdays: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM (after work/school)
- Weekends: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (morning browsing)
- Weekends: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM (evening entertainment)

Good Times (Medium Engagement):
- Weekdays: 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM (lunch break)

Avoid:
- 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM (lowest activity)
- Weekday mornings 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM (commuting to work/school)
